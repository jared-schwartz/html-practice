Giraffes

Lions

Monkeys

A monkey is a highly intelligent and agile primate, known for its playful behavior and ability to swing through trees using its long limbs and tail. They are social creatures, living in groups and exhibiting complex communication and problem-solving skills.

Alligators

Alligators are large, semi-aquatic reptiles with powerful jaws and armored bodies, typically found in freshwater environments like rivers, swamps, and marshes. Known for their stealth and strength, they are skilled hunters that have remained largely unchanged since the age of the dinosaurs.
